Interview Experience | Software Development Engineering Intern | Innovaccer

By-Utkarsh Kumar | NIT Meghalaya | Date:12-04-2021 | Innovaccer
Interview Innovaccer SDE Intern Off Campus

Innovaccer Provides its SDE Intern through its Program "Summergeeks" for 3rd year students only.There was 2 round.

Round 1(Coding round, 2 hours)

It was of 2 hours and hosted on Hackerearth.There was Problem on Ds & Algo, SQL , Aptitude ,Development ,MCQ ,Operating System.
  • 4 Coding Problem based on DS and Algo: One problem was on string (Simple),one was on matrix(maths) , one was on dp(Variation of Knapsack) and last one was on simple mathematics. Hopefully I was able to solve all 4 Problems.
  • 3-4 Question on SQL query: Queries was simple like one of them was finding 2nd max in table. I forgot all others query.
  • 1 question on Development: In this Problem we have to fetch the data from an api and we have to apply some mathematical operation on the data and print the data
  • 4-5 Mcq Based upon Aptitude: Operating System ,DS & Algo,P&C(Aptitude)

Round 2:(Interview, 40-45 minutes)

After 1 month(nearly) I got call from the HR for next round.This round was Fully Focused on Development.
  • Interviewer asked me to explain all the projects and previous internships.
  • I have used mongodb as data base in one of my project so he asked me why I have used mongodb over other database.
  • Question on "Joins" and "Acid Properties" in DBMS.
  • 1 Problem on DS & Algo. It was Range Multiplication of the numbers for q queries. Obviously he wants me to give optimal solution
  • One question on Operating System (difference b/w Process and Thread)
  • One question to explain all the OOps Concept(Encapsulation, Class ,object ,Data Hiding, Inheritance ,Abstraction ,Polymorphism etc.).

After 15-20 days I Receieved a call from hr for getting selected.
